We empower the next generation of African leaders.

The PAMBOU AZIZET Foundation mission is to increase access to quality education by improving the economic prospects of children from disadvantaged families, and ultimately reducing the economic inequality of lower income groups.

Join us today in empowering the young generation of leaders through education.

Education for all

Get children in school so they get the education and enrichment to set them up for their future.

Healthy living

Promote health & wellbeing and reduce key health risks faced by youth in the local community.

Improve livelihoods

Provide training and opportunities to create sustained livelihoods and break the cycle of poverty.

Empower girls

Grow confidence in girls and young women to open new pathways and change traditional gender beliefs.
